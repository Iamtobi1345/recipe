Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal.This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables,or meat.

Preperation time

    total: approximately 10 minutes

    preparation: 5 minutes

    cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

    2-3 large eggs

    salt, to taste

    1 tablespoon of butter or oil

    optional fillings:cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

intructions

    1. Beat the eggs: in a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they're well mixed.You can add a table spoon if water or milk for fluffier texture.

    2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.

    3. Cook the omelette: One the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.

    4. Add filling (optional) : When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omellete.

    5. Fold and serve: As the omellete continues to cook, carefully liftone edge and fold it over the filing. Let it cook for another minute, then let it slide onto the plate.

    2. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional pepper if needed.
